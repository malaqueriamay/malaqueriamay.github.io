1.BORACAY

Known as the beach capital, this tiny island has close to 12 beaches and is situated in the West Visayas region and is an ideal place for sunbathing and water activities. For people who love collecting shells, the Puka Shell Beach is meant for them. Cagban Beach is for the adventurous ones as it is near by a cave that is rumoured to still have treasure chests. For a majestic view of the island take a day trip to Mount Luho which is also the highest point. White beach is on the west side of the island and also has another small beach on its north end with the best view of the island from a platform rock located there.

2.BOHOL

Located in the central Visayas region is this Island which comprises of a UNESCO-protected site Chocolate Hills in Carmen, one of the most unique natural wonders. Bohol is home to the sanctuary for tiny Tarsier primate, known for its large engaging eyes. This island is one of the popular Philippines tourist places you should definitely visit.

3.CEBU

Another island in the Central Visayas region, if you are an underwater enthusiast then this place is known as a port to some of the best snorkeling and diving activities. Sea Caves, whale sharks, and coral reefs are amongst the things you’ll get to see. A metropolitan area with other attractions like museums and cathedrals, an hour away from the city awaits a magnificent and beautiful Sudlon National Park. In the national park, you could also do some hiking and explore.

4.BANAUE

Popular for the Batad Rice Terraces and Bangaan rice terraces which are protected by UNESCO as World Heritage sites, these are one of the most striking things to see here. The agricultural wonder is the green terraces and these look their best at sunrise. Don’t forget to take a stroll through the Banaue Ethnic Village.

5.VIGAN

A cultural blend of Spanish and Chinese influences originate from settlers of this area. This town in the Luzon region in the northern Philippines. Don’t miss a ride in a horse-drawn kalesa to experience the streets which take you through the colonial architecture. For the best view of the town, visit the Bantay Bell Tower. Amongst other tourist places in the Philippines, worth visiting are Calle Crisologo historic district and St. Paul Metropolitan Cathedral where you will come across local craftsmen and beautiful architecture. Also to ensure you live it up, you can choose a hotel stay on intermiles.com and earn miles for your stay.

6.MANILA

Known as the capital city located on Luzon Island which has colourful jeepneys as the main form of transportation and is also one of the highlights of the city. Rizal Park and San Agustin Church are popular tourist destinations that you must visit and then shop for handmade gifts and local fruits at a public market near Quiapo Church.

7.DAVAO CITY

Located in the Southern Philippine region of Mindanao this is one of the main cities with a large metropolitan area. One of the must-visit spots is Mount Apo, the highest mountain in the Philippines and amongst other places popular in the city due to natural elements are Davao Bay walk near the sea, Philippine Eagle Centre and Eden Nature Park. Abreeza Mall and SM Lanang Premier Mall are best for shopping. If you wish to explore the history and culture of the city visit the Davao Museum of History and Ethnography.

8.PUERTO PRINCESA

A coastal city that is popularly known as the hidden paradise of the Philippines. The gems of this country are the rock islands, caves, and Palawan Island. The Subterranean River National Park, another UNESCO World Heritage site is famous for a five-mile underground river and limestone caves. If you’re feeling adventurous then visit Ugong Rock to try zip lining and caving, and Honda Bay for snorkelling.

9.SAMAL

This island is for the ones who are sporty and adventurous. Also called as Garden city of Samal, you can enjoy hiking, diving, and kayaking here. You could also take a day trip to Hagimit Falls, enjoy the nature trails and tropical waterfalls. Monfort Bat Sanctuary is another place worth seeing.

10.BAGUIO

A city surrounded by mountains known as the City if of Pines as it has pine forests in abundance which you can enjoy while hiking or biking.
